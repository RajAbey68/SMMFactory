## Positioning

### You Are NOT
- Junior CSM
- Onboarding / support role
- Generic AI enthusiast

### You ARE
> **Enterprise AI Adoption Lead**  
> **Customer Success (Strategic / Enterprise)**  
> **AI Evangelist with delivery credibility**

### Core Message (use everywhere)
> "I help organisations move from AI curiosity to safe, scalable adoption — combining governance, delivery, and real-world implementation experience."

### Edge Statement
> "Unlike most AI evangelists, I've spent 20+ years delivering complex systems, so I understand where AI actually works — and where it breaks."

## Channels

### 1. LinkedIn (Primary Engine — 60% of effort)
- **3 posts per week** (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- Short, sharp, experience-led
- No generic AI hype — only real-world observations
- Goal: Build visible narrative, create pull

### 2. Direct Outreach (30% of effort)
- Target: Hiring managers, Product leaders, GTM / CS heads
- Personalised messages (not spray-and-pray)
- Goal: 10 targeted messages per week

### 3. Expert Networks (10% of effort)
- GLG, AlphaSights, Guidepoint
- Builds credibility + income while searching
- Goal: Register on 3 platforms, accept 2+ consultations
