### Creative Rules (from Strategy Foundations)
- ✅ Lo-fi / authentic content > polished studio shots (Meta 2026 trend)
- ✅ Mobile-shot video performs BETTER than professional for Advantage+ campaigns
- ✅ Show REAL experiences — actual guests, actual meals, actual sunsets
- ✅ Keep text on images to <20% (Meta still penalizes text-heavy creative)
- ✅ Every ad is a SALESPERSON — it must contain benefit + proof + CTA (Tobias Allen Rule)
